WEIRD Lab

Washington Embodied Intelligence and Robotics Development Lab

We work on algorithms and systems that enable real-world robotic manipulation in the home. Our interests also span computer vision, language modeling, human-robot interaction and broader notions of robustness and reliability in machine learning systems. We are affiliated with UW Robotics and the Paul G. Allen School for Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington.
